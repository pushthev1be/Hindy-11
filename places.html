The Directory

Places

Where we eat, shop, gather and worship. African-owned spots around Indianapolis, kept by the community — if we're missing one, tell us.

  • Kalakutah Republic Grill

    Food

    Suya, asun, pepper soup, jollof and swallow — named for Fela's republic, and one of the best-known Nigerian kitchens in the city. Weekends run late.

    3832 Eagle View Dr, Indianapolis, IN 46254 (317) 665-8071
    Website →

  • Miami's Garden

    Food

    Restaurant and lounge on the northwest side — Nigerian and Caribbean plates, hookah and Afrobeats nights. Friday to Sunday, evenings till late.

    7102 Woodland Dr, Indianapolis, IN 46278 (317) 969-7828
    Website →

  • K-Arise Store

    Market

    The northwest-side stop for the pantry run — fresh yam, garri, egusi, crayfish, smoked fish, frozen foods and household imports.

    4022 N High School Rd, Indianapolis, IN 46254 (317) 925-1283
    Website →

  • Victory Super Store African Foods

    Market

    West-side African supermarket — Nigerian and Ghanaian staples, with wholesale quantities when you're cooking for a hall.

    7460 Rockville Rd, Indianapolis, IN 46214 (317) 998-2510
    Map →

  • Olas Villa

    Events

    Nigerian-owned banquet hall for weddings, birthdays, naming ceremonies and owambes — a hall and a club area you can book separately or together.

    6450 W 10th St, Suite 5, Indianapolis, IN 46214
    Website →

  • Paradox Lounge

    Events

    Fountain Square lounge behind many of the city's Afrobeats nights, day parties and diaspora events. Thursday to Sunday.

    1059 Virginia Ave, Indianapolis, IN 46203
    Map →

  • RCCG Rod of God Parish

    Faith

    Redeemed Christian Church of God parish on the west side — Sunday services and midweek programmes.

    5350 Allied Blvd, Indianapolis, IN 46224 (317) 418-7388
    Website →

  • RCCG Covenant House

    Faith

    RCCG parish on Lafayette Road, right in the heart of the city's African business corridor.

    5310 Lafayette Rd, Indianapolis, IN 46254 (317) 726-0889
    Website →
