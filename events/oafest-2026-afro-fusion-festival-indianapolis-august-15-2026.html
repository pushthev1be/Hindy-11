OAFEST 2026 brings the Afro Fusion Festival to Indy

The Oodua Progressive Union (OPU) USA presents a full day of African heritage, music, culture and community at the Garnet Waterfront — free and open to everyone.

When
Saturday, August 15, 2026 · 12:00 Noon (US time)
Where
Garnet Waterfront Event Center · 2930 Waterfront Parkway W Drive, Indianapolis, IN
Admission
Free & open to the public — everyone is welcome
Hosted by
Oodua Progressive Union (OPU) USA
RSVP
OPU USA General Secretary — OAFEST

Join us for an unforgettable celebration of African heritage, leadership, culture, unity and community at OAFEST 2026. Under the banner Common Heritage · One Destiny, the OPU USA Afro Fusion Festival is more than an event — it is a movement to build community, preserve heritage and inspire the future.

What to experience

  • Traditional music & African dance
  • Authentic cuisine
  • Arts & crafts and cultural exhibitions
  • Leadership conversations & business networking
  • Youth engagement
  • Family entertainment & community fellowship

Who's leading it

  • Amb. Niyi Aluko — Festival Director
  • Princess Adebisi Ostine — Indiana Organizing Chairperson & Host State Director
  • High Chief Olami Omagbiyi Anthony “Boyede Greenlyte” — Festival Operations Director

Families, organizations, businesses and friends of Africa are invited to take part as sponsors, exhibitors, volunteers, vendors, performers and supporters. Every act of support helps preserve our heritage and strengthens our community.
