OAFEST 2026 — the Afro Fusion Festival

Common heritage, one destiny. OPU USA brings a full day of African culture, music, fashion, food and unity to the Indianapolis waterfront — and every generation is invited.

When
Saturday, August 15, 2026 · 12:00 noon
Where
Garnet Waterfront Event Center · 2930 Waterfront Parkway W Drive, Indianapolis
Cost
Free & open to the public — everyone is welcome
Host
Oodua Progressive Union (OPU) USA
RSVP
OPU USA General Secretary — OAFEST

OAFEST is more than a festival — it's a movement that brings generations together, celebrates our identity, and proudly tells the African story to the world. Expect breathtaking cultural performances, the rhythm of the drums, tradition celebrated in style, and a day built for families, elders and the next generation alike.

What to expect

Traditional music · African dance · authentic cuisine · arts & crafts · cultural exhibitions · leadership conversations · business networking · youth engagement · family entertainment · community fellowship.

Get involved

OPU USA is welcoming sponsors, exhibitors, volunteers, vendors, performers and supporters. Every act of support helps preserve our heritage and strengthens the community — reach the organizers through the RSVP contact above.

Common Heritage. One Destiny. Oodua Agbe Wa! 🙏🏾
