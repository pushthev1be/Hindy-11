Omoboi wants you to “Let Go”

Smooth Afro-R&B for the late-night drives — the single to know this week.

By The Hindy-eleven desk · Jul 16, 2026 · 2 min read

Omoboi’s "Let Go" arrives as the perfect late-night soundtrack — a smooth, moody Afro-R&B track that settles into the space between a whisper and a groove. The production breathes, giving the vocalist room to glide over hypnotic beats, capturing that rare moment when a song feels both introspective and irresistible.

The sound

Built on layered synths and a steady pulse, "Let Go" showcases Omoboi’s ability to craft intimate moments within a larger soundscape. The track draws from contemporary R&B sensibilities while staying rooted in the afrobeats tradition—it’s the kind of single that works equally well on headphones at 2 AM or as the soundtrack to someone’s evening drive through Indianapolis.
