Kelvo’s Best of Summer 2025

The Indianapolis DJ curates his perfect blend of afrobeat, amapiano & afrohouse — the soundtrack for the season.

By The Hindy-eleven desk · Jul 17, 2026 · 2 min read

As summer peaks, DJ Kelvo brings together the season’s essential sounds — a carefully curated mix of afrobeat, amapiano and afrohouse that moves between late-night drives and weekend gatherings. This mixtape is his statement on what’s moving in 2025.

The sound

Kelvo’s mix blends the smooth grooves of contemporary afrobeat with the infectious energy of amapiano and the hypnotic depth of afrohouse. It’s a journey through the African diaspora sound that’s defining the summer, built for both focused listening and background energy.
